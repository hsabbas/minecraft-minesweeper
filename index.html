Creeper Sweeper!
Dig down to find ore, but beware of creepers!
They are attracted to rare ore, and the rarer the ore, the more creepers there are around it.
Use the guide on the right to see how many creepers there are surrounding different kinds of ore.
Luckily, creepers are afraid of cats.
Leave a cat where you think there is a creeper by right clicking the block.
You get one cat for every creeper on the board.
